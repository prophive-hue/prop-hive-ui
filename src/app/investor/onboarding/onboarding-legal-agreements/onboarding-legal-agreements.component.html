INVESTOR RISK DISCLOSURE STATEMENT

Last Updated: May 17, 2025

This Risk Disclosure Statement outlines the key risks associated with property investments through our Platform. Please read this document carefully before making any investment decisions.

1. GENERAL INVESTMENT RISKS

1.1 Capital Risk
Your capital is at risk. The value of your investment may go down as well as up, and you may not get back the amount you invested.

1.2 No Guaranteed Returns
Returns on investments are not guaranteed and may vary from projections. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

1.3 Liquidity Risk
Property investments are generally illiquid. You may not be able to sell your investment quickly or at your desired price.

2. PROPERTY-SPECIFIC RISKS

2.1 Market Risk
Property values can fluctuate due to economic conditions, interest rate changes, or local market factors.

2.2 Development Risks
For development projects, risks include construction delays, cost overruns, contractor issues, and failure to secure necessary permits.

2.3 Rental Income Risks
Rental income may be affected by vacancy periods, tenant defaults, market demand, and changes in rental rates.

2.4 Property Damage
Properties may suffer damage from various causes, including natural disasters, fire, or vandalism, which may not be fully covered by insurance.

3. PLATFORM-RELATED RISKS

3.1 Technology Risks
The Platform may experience technical issues or security breaches that could affect your investments.

3.2 Operational Risks
Risks associated with the day-to-day operations of the Platform, including processing errors or administrative failures.

4. LEGAL AND REGULATORY RISKS

4.1 Regulatory Changes
Changes in laws or regulations governing property investments or crowdfunding may adversely affect your investment.

4.2 Tax Risks
Tax laws and their interpretation may change, potentially affecting the net returns on your investment.

5. DIVERSIFICATION NOTICE

It is advisable not to invest more than 10% of your investment portfolio in any single property or property-type. Diversification across different properties and asset classes is recommended.

6. SEEK INDEPENDENT ADVICE

We recommend seeking independent financial, legal, and tax advice before making investment decisions.

7. ACKNOWLEDGMENT

- You have read and understood this Risk Disclosure Statement
- You understand that property investment involves significant risks
- You are investing based on your own judgment and at your own risk
- You acknowledge that the Platform does not provide investment advice

1.1 The Investor agrees to subscribe for an investment interest in the selected property project(s) as specified during the investment process.

1.2 Each investment is subject to minimum and maximum investment amounts as specified for the particular property project.

1.3 By executing this Agreement, the Investor commits to providing the full investment amount by the specified deadline.

2. INVESTMENT STRUCTURE

2.1 Investment Vehicle
Investments are made through a special purpose vehicle (SPV) structured as a company or trust specifically established for the property project.

2.2 Ownership Interest
The Investor's ownership interest in the property project will be proportional to their investment amount relative to the total amount raised.

2.3 Documentation
The Investor will receive a certificate or digital confirmation of their investment shares or units after the investment is processed.

3. INVESTMENT PROCESS

3.1 Reservation
The Investor's funds will be held in an escrow account until the funding target for the property project is achieved.

3.2 Investment Confirmation
Once the funding target is met, the Investor will receive confirmation that their investment has been finalized.

3.3 Failed Funding
If the funding target is not reached within the specified timeframe, all funds will be returned to the Investor without interest.

4. DISTRIBUTIONS AND RETURNS

4.1 Distribution Schedule
Returns will be distributed according to the schedule specified in the property project documentation.

4.2 Payment Method
Distributions will be made directly to the Investor's registered bank account.

4.3 Taxation
Distributions may be subject to applicable taxes, which are the responsibility of the Investor.

5. TERM AND EXIT

5.1 Investment Term
The investment term is as specified in the property project documentation.

5.2 Exit Mechanism
The primary exit strategy will be as outlined in the property project documentation, typically through property sale or refinancing

5.3 Early Exit
Early exit options, if available, will be subject to specific terms, conditions, and potential fees as described in the property project documentation.
